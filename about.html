Welcome to Novel Nest

Founded in 2018, Novel Nest began as a small community of book lovers who wanted to create a space where readers and writers could connect. What started as a passion project has grown into one of the most vibrant literary communities online.

Our platform is dedicated to celebrating the art of storytelling in all its forms. Whether you're an avid reader looking for your next favorite book or an aspiring writer seeking an audience for your work, Novel Nest is your home.

We believe that everyone has a story to tell, and we're committed to providing the tools and community support to help those stories find their voice.

Our Mission

At Novel Nest, we're on a mission to democratize storytelling. We want to break down the barriers that prevent talented writers from sharing their work and make literature accessible to everyone, everywhere.

We envision a world where every voice can be heard, where stories from diverse backgrounds and perspectives are celebrated, and where the love of reading and writing brings people together.
