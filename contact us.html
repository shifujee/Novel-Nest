Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my story to Novel Nest?

You can submit your story by visiting our "Create Story" page. You'll need to create an account first if you don't already have one. Once logged in, you can fill out the submission form with your story details and content.

How long does it take to get a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days. For technical support issues, we aim to respond within 24 hours. During peak times, response times may be slightly longer.

Can I publish my story anonymously?

Yes, you can choose to publish under a pen name. However, our team will still need your real information for account verification purposes, which will remain confidential.

Do you offer publishing services for physical books?

Currently, Novel Nest is focused on digital storytelling. However, we may introduce print-on-demand services in the future. Many of our authors have successfully used their exposure on our platform to secure traditional publishing deals.

How can I report inappropriate content?

Each story page has a "Report" button where you can flag content that violates our guidelines. For urgent matters, please email our moderation team at moderation@novelnest.com with details of the concern.
